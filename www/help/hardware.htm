Orange connector:

  • analog mode: (5.1, Audigy) Center (left), Subwoofer (right)

  • digital mode:
            A) left signal: (1024, 5.1, Audigy): spdifo0 (digital front)
            B) right signal:
                    I. (1024): spdifo3 (digital rear) or spdifo1 [11] (front&rear mix) [12]
                    II. (5.1, Audigy); spdifo3 (digital rear)

  • third signal: (5.1, Audigy): digital center/subwoofer (spdifo2 [13])

Note: SPDIF#1 is SPDIFO0; SPDIF#2 is SPDIFO3; third signal not shown (should be before 'GND' for 5.1 and Audigy cards)

Input/Output limitations (according to official PDFs):

  • Line2/Mic2 cannot be used simultaneously with onboard i2s.
  • AUX2 cannot be used simultaneously with Optical/Coaxial SPDIF in (except in Bypass mode).

Daughterboards:

Digital I/O Card (original, model CT4660):

  • midi in
  • midi out
  • coaxial RCA in
  • coaxial RCA out
  • digital DIN

Optical Digital I/O Card (model CT4770):

  • LineIn2 (*)
  • Digital I/O (for Digital I/O module)
  • digital DIN
  • midi in
  • midi out

(*) available only if connected via AUD_EXT

Digital I/O Module (model CT4800): -- connected to Digital I/O

  • optical in
  • optical out
  • coaxial in
  • coaxial out

Digital Output Module (model CT4801): -- connected to 'Orange Jack'

  • coaxial output (spdifo 0) -- front
  • optical output (spdifo 0) -- front
  • coaxial output (spdifo 2(?)) rear
  • optical output (spdifo 2(?)) rear

LiveDrive (CT4860), LiveDrive II (CT4861), LiveDrive IR (SB0010), AudigyDrive (?):

  • coaxial input
  • coaxial output
  • optical input (*)
  • optical output (*)
  • AUX2 input (*)
  • headphones
  • mic2/line2
  • midi in
  • midi out
  • sb1394 (Audigy only)
  • IR (LiveDrive IR, Audigy Drive)

(*) available on LiveDrive II and later models
